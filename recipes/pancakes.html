Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a traditional favourite loved by all. This is a recipe for thin Swedish pancakes served with jam, whipped cream or ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 3 dl Flour
  • 6 dl Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoons Salt
  • 2 tablespoons Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour into half of the milk until it forms a smooth batter. Then whisk in the remaining milk, eggs and salt.
  2. Let the batter rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Melt the butter and whisk it into the batter. Fry the batter in a pan into thin pancakes.
