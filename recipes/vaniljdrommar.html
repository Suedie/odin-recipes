Vaniljdrömmar

Description

Vaniljdrömmar are crunchy Swedish cookies filled with air.

Ingredients

  • 50 g Butter
  • 1 1/2 dl Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Vanilla sugar
  • 1/2 dl Rapeseed oil
  • 1/2 tablespoons Baker's ammonia
  • 2 dl flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 C.
  2. Whisk butter, sugar and vanilla sugar into a batter. Gradually add the oil during this process. Add the baker's ammonia with some flour and whisk it in. Then gradually add the rest of the flour.
  3. Roll the dough into small balls. Put them on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the middle of the oven for 15-20 minutes. Then let cool on the baking sheet.
