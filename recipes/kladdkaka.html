Kladdkaka

Description

Kladdkaka is a sticky Swedish chocolate cake, similar to brownies.

Ingredients

  • 100 g Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 1/2 dl Sugar
  • 3 tablespoons Cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoon Vanilla sugar
  • 1 1/2 dl flour
  • A pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 C.
  2. Grease a cake pan.
  3. Melt the butter and let it cool for a bit.
  4. Whisk together the eggs and sugar.
  5. Mix together the cocoa, vanilla sugar, flour and salt, then add it to the egg and sugar mix.
  6. Carefully add and whisk in the melted butter into a smooth batter. Then pour the batter into the cake pan.
  7. Bake in the middle of the oven for 10-15 minutes. Then let the cake cool.
  8. Optionally sift some powdered sugar on top as a garnish.
